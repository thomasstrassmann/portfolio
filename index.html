About me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio homepage!
I'm Thomas, a Web Developer from Germany and I've recently turned my hobby into my profession, as I'm a certified web developer with a diploma in full stack development.

Before that, I worked as a key account manager / head of sales in the digital field for a long time. However, at the end of a working day I never really saw what I had accomplished or achieved. In the field of web development, it's a different story: What drives me is creating things out of nothing & seeing instant results. Solving problems is my passion!
